Simplifying Smart Home Control: Redesigning MyLeviton for Ease and Efficiency

This UX/UI redesign focused on modernizing MyLeviton, a smart-home app that allows users to control their lighting. The goal was to enhance usability, simplify home light connectivity, and improve navigation for a smoother, more intuitive experience. Research involved analyzing user interaction patterns and feedback to streamline page layouts and increase accessibility, delivering on the client's vision for a user-friendly, efficient app interface that empowers users to manage their home lighting with ease.

Navigating Complex Challenges to Deliver a Streamlined Smart-Home Experience

The redesign journey involved thorough competitor analysis and experimentation to create an intuitive interface for MyLeviton, though initial stages proved challenging. The project evolved gradually, with significant adjustments to page organization, information layout, and design aesthetics to enhance usability. While feedback focused on refining colour schemes and modernizing the overall look, no major revisions were needed. The client was satisfied with the final design, which now offers a more accessible and cohesive smart-home experience.

A Refined Experience: Clean, Modern, and Engaging

The redesign achieved a striking contrast between the outdated, cluttered original and the sleek, modernized version. Prioritizing user experience and thorough research led to a seamless transformation, making the website more visually appealing and functional. The client was pleased with the final product, and the process confirmed that focusing on modernization and user-first design is key to successful outcomes in future projects.
